Sport at University of Bristol

Sport at Bristol

BUCS: Bristol is currently ranked third in the British University & College Sports league table! One of the reasons for Bristol's high scoring position is the sheer number of clubs that take part to represent the university. No matter what you're interested in, there will definitely be a sport society for you!

Competitive Sport: Many of our sports teams play at an extremely high level, competing with non-university teams on the weekend. If you are interested in continuing a sport that you have excelled at before joining the university then please contact the relevant club captain to see what Bristol can do to support you!

Why play sport?

There are many reasons to take up a sport at university, these are a few of the main ones!


TRAVEL

Many of our sports clubs at Bristol host sports tours around the UK and beyond!

HEALTH

By carrying out regular exercise you will stay much healthier, join a sports club to make working out fun!

FRIENDS

Playing a sport at uni is without a doubt one of the best way to meet new friends that you will keep in touch with for life!
